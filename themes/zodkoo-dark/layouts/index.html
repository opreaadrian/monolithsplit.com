You're losing business to your competition!

You're throwing money out the window!

Have you ever wondered how is it that your competition is always ahead of you? You probably thought it's because they have bigger budgets, better developers or better marketing staff. They might even have a saurcerer somewhere.
You experimented with bigger budgets and increasing the headcount. All you ended up doing was wasting more money.

Take a look at companies like Amazon, Netflix or Google. They have amazing R&D budgets and a huge talent pool. They don't use that sheer force to push things in the right direction. They work smarter. Ever heard of Amazon's 2-pizza-teams? That's what they use to develop things like Amazon Web Services (AWS).

There is a better way!

Stop adding features to that old monolith. Start implementing microservices.

That's right! Although it sounds like nonsense, stopping the feature-bloat on your current application is the way to make more money. Start splitting that huge chunk of code into money-making services, architected around your business!

Remember that mobile application you wanted to build? Did you end up dropping it because it involved too much work? With microservices you can think of a mobile app, a web app or a desktop app as representations of your data! You could even create a command line interface for the terminal-loving clients!

There's just no room for 6-month release cycles. Technology is moving too fast and changing the world around us. With companies deploying multiple times a day, it's hard not to be left behind.

Microservices enable you to deploy as many times you like and perform experiments that would otherwise seem impossible due to the big "blast radius" in a monolithic architecture.
